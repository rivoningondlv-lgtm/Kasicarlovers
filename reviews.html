How to Wash Your Car Like a pro (Guide)

Two bucket method

The two-bucket method is a popular car washing technique that helps minimize the risk of scratching your car's paint. Here's how to do it:

  1. Fill one bucket with soapy water (using a car wash soap) and the other bucket with clean water for rinsing.
  2. Start by rinsing your car with water to remove loose dirt and debris.
  3. Dip your wash mitt or sponge into the soapy water, then wash a section of your car using gentle, straight-line motions.
  4. After washing each section, rinse the mitt or sponge in the clean water bucket to remove dirt before dipping it back into the soapy water.
  5. Continue this process until you've washed the entire car.
  6. Rinse your car thoroughly with clean water to remove all soap residue.
  7. Dry your car with a microfiber towel or chamois to prevent water spots.
