About Kasicarlovers

Kasicarlovers is a platform created by two passionate car enthusiasts from South Africa. Our mission is to share our love for cars, explore the automotive culture in our country, and connect with fellow car lovers.

Our Story

The "Kasicarlovers" website was started by two childhood friends, Donald and Lesiba , who grew up in the townships and shared a deep passion for the unique South African car scene.

They spent their weekends learning about classic local Rides and the vibrant customizing culture, and they started this site to share that tradition with the world.

Their dream is to document the best Mzansi's car history, from the legends of the 80s to the latest street builds. This is more than just a hobby; it's a way to keep the kasi car culture alive and thriving.

What We Do

  • Car Reviews: We provide honest and detailed reviews of various car models, focusing on their performance, design, and features.
  • Road Trips: Join us as we explore scenic routes and hidden gems across South Africa, sharing tips and highlights from our journeys.
  • Car Maintenance Tips: We offer practical advice on how to keep your car in top condition, from basic maintenance to advanced care.
  • Interviews: We connect with other car enthusiasts and industry experts to bring you insights and stories from the automotive world.
  • Events: Stay updated on upcoming car events and gatherings in South Africa that you won't want to miss.

Join Us

We invite you to join us on this exciting journey. Whether you're a seasoned car enthusiast or just starting to explore the world of cars, there's something for everyone at Kasicarlovers. Follow us for the latest updates, reviews, and adventures!
